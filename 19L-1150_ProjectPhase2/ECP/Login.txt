Valid ECP
Username: alphanumeric characters, between 5-20 characters in length.
Password: alphanumeric characters, at least 8 characters long with at least one uppercase letter and one special character.
Email Address: a valid email address format, unique and not already registered on the site.
Invalid ECP
Invalid email format, such as missing "@" or ".com" at the end
Username already exists in the system
Password and Confirm Password fields do not match
Invalid date of birth entered

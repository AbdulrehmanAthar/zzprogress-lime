Valid invoice Details: valid invoice details such as invoice number, date, due date, line items, quantity, and price.
Valid Client Details: valid client details such as name, address, email, and phone number.
Valid Payment Methods: valid payment methods accepted by Odo.com, such as credit card, PayPal, bank transfer, and so on.
Invalid ECP'S
Providing incorrect client information while creating an invoice.
Entering an invalid amount or format while creating an invoice.
Trying to send an invoice without internet connectivity.
Entering a past date as the invoice due date.
